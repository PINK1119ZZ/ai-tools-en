ExpressVPN remains one of the most polished, reliable VPNs money can buy in 2026. Its core strength is consistency: wherever you are in the world, ExpressVPN tends to just work — whether you're trying to stream BBC iPlayer from Tokyo, access a US business tool from Southeast Asia, or simply browse privately from a hotel in a high-censorship country.

Lightway protocol is ExpressVPN's answer to WireGuard — a from-scratch TLS-based protocol that uses wolfSSL for cryptography. It's fast and has excellent reconnect behavior, which matters on mobile where you're switching between WiFi and cellular. Lightway is open-sourced and audited, which builds trust.

Router support is where ExpressVPN genuinely shines over NordVPN. ExpressVPN offers its own custom router firmware (Aircove) and supports more router models out of the box. If you want to protect every device on your home or office network — including smart TVs, gaming consoles, and IoT devices — ExpressVPN is the easier choice.

Streaming access remains ExpressVPN's killer feature. Its MediaStreamer DNS service unlocks streaming platforms even on devices that can't run a VPN app (like older smart TVs). For travel-heavy users who need reliable access to entertainment from 20+ countries, ExpressVPN maintains the most robust unblocking across Netflix libraries, Disney+, Hulu, BBC iPlayer, and regional platforms.

Privacy & Security: Does It Actually Hold Up?

Both NordVPN and ExpressVPN have passed the most important test a VPN can face: real-world legal requests. NordVPN was seized by Estonian authorities in 2018 (a server in their network was compromised by a third-party data center). Result: nothing useful recovered, because nothing was stored. ExpressVPN had a server seized in Turkey during the Russian ambassador assassination investigation in 2017. Same result: no usable logs.

Beyond these real-world incidents, both providers have commissioned multiple independent audits:

  • NordVPN: Audited by PricewaterhouseCoopers (2018, 2020) and Deloitte (2023). All audits confirmed the no-logs policy. RAM-only infrastructure added in 2020 ensures nothing persists through a server reboot.
  • ExpressVPN: Audited by KPMG (2022) confirming no-logs compliance. Their Lightway protocol code is open-sourced and audited separately by Cure53.

For jurisdiction: NordVPN operates from Panama, outside both the 5 Eyes and 14 Eyes intelligence alliances. ExpressVPN is registered in the British Virgin Islands — also outside 5/9/14 Eyes, though the BVI has slightly closer ties to UK law enforcement than Panama does.

⚠️ Important: A VPN is not anonymity

NordVPN and ExpressVPN protect your traffic from local network snoopers (cafés, ISPs, public WiFi) and can help obscure your IP. They do not make you fully anonymous online. For business use, the key value is protecting credential theft on untrusted networks and maintaining access to geo-restricted services — not circumventing legal processes.

FAQ

Is NordVPN faster than ExpressVPN in 2026?

Yes, in most benchmarks. NordVPN's NordLynx protocol (built on WireGuard) delivers average speeds of 730–950 Mbps on a gigabit connection, compared to ExpressVPN's 680–820 Mbps via Lightway. The difference is marginal for most users but measurable.

Which is cheaper: NordVPN or ExpressVPN?

NordVPN is significantly cheaper. The 2-year plan runs approximately $3.09–$3.99/month, while ExpressVPN's best annual rate is ~$6.67/month. Over two years, NordVPN saves you roughly $80–$90.

Which VPN is better for streaming in 2026?

ExpressVPN has a slight edge for streaming variety across many countries and regions. NordVPN reliably unblocks US Netflix, BBC iPlayer, Hulu, and Amazon Prime but can be inconsistent with some smaller regional libraries. For streaming as the primary use case across 20+ countries, ExpressVPN wins.

Does NordVPN keep logs?

No. NordVPN operates a strict no-logs policy independently audited by PricewaterhouseCoopers and Deloitte. Its RAM-only server infrastructure means nothing persists after a reboot. The company is headquartered in Panama, outside 5/9/14 Eyes intelligence alliances.

How many devices does NordVPN allow?

NordVPN allows 10 simultaneous device connections per account, compared to ExpressVPN's 8. NordVPN also supports router-level installation that covers every device on your network under a single connection.

Which VPN should freelancers and remote workers use?

NordVPN is the stronger choice for freelancers and remote workers — better price, more devices, dedicated IP add-on for whitelisting client servers, and Threat Protection that blocks malware even without activating the VPN tunnel. The ~$85 savings over 2 years compared to ExpressVPN is meaningful too.
