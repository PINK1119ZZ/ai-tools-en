Can a one-person business realistically use AI to replace a team?

Increasingly yes. In 2026, a solopreneur using Systeme.io for email + funnels, CapCut AI for video, DataCamp for skill-building, and Cloudways for fast hosting can execute workflows that previously required a 3–5 person team. Is Systeme.io good for solopreneurs?
